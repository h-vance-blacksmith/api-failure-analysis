# Help Center: Troubleshooting Connection & API Errors

> **Audience:** Customers / End-Users
> **Goal:** Help our users quickly identify why they might be seeing an error and how to provide our Support team with the details needed for a fast fix.

---

## 👋 We're here to help
If you've encountered an "API Error," "Connection Failed," or "Unexpected Behavior" message, we know how frustrating that can be. This guide will walk you through a few quick steps to get things back on track.

### 1. The Quick Fix Checklist
Before reaching out, try these three steps that resolve 80% of common issues:
- **Refresh the Page:** Sometimes a temporary connection "hiccup" can cause an error.
- **Check Your Internet:** Ensure you have a stable connection (especially if you're on a VPN or public Wi-Fi).
- **Try "Incognito" or "Private" Mode:** This helps determine if a browser extension or old cache is interfering with the service.

---

## 2. "How to Help Us Help You"
If the quick fixes didn't work, our Support team will need a little "detective work" from your side to find the root cause. Providing these three things in your first message can save hours of back-and-forth!

### A. The "Full Screen" Screenshot
Instead of just the error message, please take a screenshot of your **entire browser window**.
- **Why?** It helps us see the URL, any other open tabs that might be relevant, and the exact placement of the error.

### B. The Browser "Inspector" (For advanced help)
If our team asks for a "Console Log" or a "Network Trace," here is how you can get it for us (it only takes 10 seconds!):
1. **Right-click** anywhere on our page and select **"Inspect."**
2. A window will open on the side. Click the tab that says **"Console."**
3. If you see any **RED text**, please take a screenshot and attach it to your ticket.

### C. The "What, When, and Where"
- **What** were you doing the moment the error appeared? (e.g., "I clicked the 'Save' button on the Profile page").
- **When** did it happen? (Include your timezone!).
- **Where** did it happen? (Include the full web address/URL of the page).

---

## 3. What Happens Next?
Once you submit your ticket with the details above, here is our promise to you:
- **Triage (1-2 Hours):** A Technical Support Engineer will review your evidence and try to reproduce the issue.
- **Investigation:** We will check our internal "health monitors" to see if there is a broader system issue.
- **Resolution:** We will either provide a quick fix, a workaround, or escalate the issue to our Engineering team for a permanent patch.
